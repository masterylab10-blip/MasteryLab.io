Education Silver

(Weekend 1) Fundamentals

1. Foundation / Beginner Teacher Level

Focus: Solid basics + body mechanics

This level is about building correct technique so you can later teach it safely and clearly.

What's covered:

  • Bachata Sensual basics (basic step, side steps, weight transfer)
  • Body Isolation fundamentals (chest, hips, torso)
  • Basic waves (body wave, head position basics)
  • Frame, connection & leading/following fundamentals
  • Musicality basics (timing, phrasing)
  • Teaching basics: how to explain movements clearly
For teachers: You're learning how to move correctly and how to explain it to students without causing bad habits or injuries.

Bachata Sensual Professionals

Education Bronze

(Weekend 2) Technique & Methodology

2. Intermediate / Technique & Methodology Level

Focus: Clean technique + pedagogy (how to teach)

This is where you stop being "just a good dancer" and start becoming a good teacher.

What's covered:

  • Advanced waves (circular waves, chest-to-hip waves)
  • Head movements & rolls (done safely)
  • Counterbalance, elasticity & connection quality
  • Transitions between sensual and traditional bachata
  • Partnering technique (lead/follow clarity)
  • Musical interpretation & styling
  • Teaching methodology:
    • How to structure a class
    • How to correct students
    • Common mistakes & how to fix them
For teachers: You're learning why movements work and how to break them down for different learning styles.

Bachata Sensual Professionals

Education Gold

(Weekend 3) Professional Teaching / Mastery

3. Advanced / Professional Teacher Level

Focus: Mastery, safety, class design & artistry

This is where teachers develop a recognizable quality and identity.

What's covered:

  • Advanced partner mechanics (elasticity, rotation, counter-rotation)
  • Safe execution of dips, cambres & head movements
  • Complex sensual combinations & transitions
  • Musicality on a high level (accents, breaks, texture in music)
  • Injury prevention & anatomy awareness
  • Class design:
    • Curriculum building
    • Progressive lesson planning
    • Teaching large groups vs private lessons
  • Teacher presence, communication & class energy
For teachers: This level is about teaching safely, beautifully, and professionally, not just dancing well.
