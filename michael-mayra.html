BEYOND TYPICAL WORKSHOPS

For the very first time in Switzerland, internationally renowned artists Michael & Myra present an exclusive Bachata Technique Lab in Basel!

This intensive weekend goes beyond typical workshops. Instead of choreography-focused classes, you will experience an academic and structured approach to Bachata technique, aimed at refining understanding, body mechanics, and quality of movement.

THE M&M METHOD

Michael & Myra are known worldwide not only for their artistry, but for their precision, analytical teaching style, and their unique M&M Technique and Dance Healing concept.

They help students unlock tension, gain awareness, and build unshakeable confidence in their bodies. This format is designed for dancers seeking deep learning, clearer body intelligence, and a more refined, academic understanding of Bachata movement.
