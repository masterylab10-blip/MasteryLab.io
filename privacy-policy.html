Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

MasteryLab ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and process your information in compliance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

The responsible body for data processing on this website is:

MasteryLab
Olten, Switzerland
Email: labmastery@outlook.com

2. Collection and Processing of Personal Data

We collect personal data that you provide to us voluntarily when registering for our workshops, labs, or contacting us. This includes:

  • Contact Information (Name, Email, Phone/WhatsApp, Address)
  • Dance Role (Leader/Follower) & Experience Level
  • Video submissions (for teacher track applications)
  • Payment Information (processed securely by third-party processors)

The processing is based on the performance of a contract (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR / nFADP equiv.) or on legitimate interests (e.g., event organization, safety).

3. Use of Personal Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Processing your registration and participation in events.
  • Communicating updates, schedule changes, or important information about the event.
  • Evaluating applications for advanced/teacher tracks (via video submissions).
  • Processing payments and invoicing.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data. However, we may share necessary data with trusted service providers to operate our services:

  • Google Workspace (Drive/Sheets): For storing registration data and video submissions.
  • Stripe / TicketTailor: For secure payment processing.
  • Supabase: For user authentication and profile management.
  • FormSubmit: For handling form submissions securely.

When data is transferred outside Switzerland (e.g., to the USA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or reliance on the Swiss-US Data Privacy Framework.

5. Photos and Videos at Events

Please note that photos and videos may be taken during our events for promotional purposes. By attending, you consent to being filmed or photographed. If you wish to opt-out, please inform us in writing prior to the event.

6. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or manipulation. This includes SSL encryption on our website and restricted access to personal data within our organization.

7. Your Rights

Under Swiss law (nFADP), you have the right to:

  • Request information about your stored personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (provided no legal retention obligations exist).
  • Object to data processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at labmastery@outlook.com.

8. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience (e.g., session management). You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version published on our website applies.
